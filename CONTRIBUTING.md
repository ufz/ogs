# How to contribute

Please refer to our [Developer Guide](https://www.opengeosys.org/stable/docs/devguide) for
instructions on contributing to OpenGeoSys:

- The [Getting Started](https://www.opengeosys.org/stable/docs/devguide/getting-started/introduction/)-section
  guides you through setting up a development environment.
- In [Development Workflows](https://www.opengeosys.org/stable/docs/devguide/development-workflows/branching-model/)
  you learn about our development model.
